## [2026-05-15] v1.2.0

### Added

- `qbr-narrative` — The marketing leader's QBR narrative builder. Replaces 2 days of slide assembly with a 90-second narrative draft (what happened / what it means / what we're changing) plus a board-ready Slack version. Includes the "question leadership is most likely to ask" line that pre-empts the hard questions before they hit the room. Companion skill to `weekly-marketing-pulse` — same Skill philosophy, quarterly cadence. Includes starter template for teaching/sharing alongside the production SKILL.md.

### Changed

- Skill count: 28 → 29
- README: Added `qbr-narrative` row under Operations & Reporting
